An operational story

An easier way to manage product truth

DataPlus was created around a simple problem: marketplace work gets messy when product details, vendor files, SKU variations, dimensions, weights, channel rules, and export templates all live in different places.

The system is shaped for real catalog work. It helps teams inspect the source dump, enrich products, review missing data, prepare exports, and keep marketplace-ready information moving without losing sight of where the data came from.
