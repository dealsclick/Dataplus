DataPlus Privacy Policy

DataPlus is an internal catalog and marketplace operations tool used by LINQ USA CORP.

Information used

When connected to marketplace accounts, DataPlus may access order, buyer shipping, product, and fulfillment information needed to manage orders and catalog operations.

How information is stored

DataPlus stores imported data in the local database controlled by LINQ USA CORP. Marketplace credentials and tokens are stored locally and are used only to connect to authorized marketplace APIs.

How information is shared

DataPlus does not sell customer information. Information is used for order processing, catalog management, shipping, customer support, accounting, and marketplace compliance.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact LINQ USA CORP through the business contact information associated with the marketplace account.
